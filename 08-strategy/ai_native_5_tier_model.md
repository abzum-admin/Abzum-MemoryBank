## Narrative
### Structure
5-tier human structure supporting 95% AI agent workforce. T1: 3-8 humans (Strategic Command). T2: 25-45 humans (AI Platform - CRITICAL). T3: 15-25 humans (AI Governance). T4: 20-40 humans (Human Interface). T5: 10-20 humans (Corporate). Total: 80-150 humans.

### Dependencies
Design principle: Humans exist only to direct agents (set objectives), govern agents (set limits), and represent company where legal standing or human trust is non-delegable.

### Highlights
Tier 2 concentration risk: if this team fails, 95% of operations halt. 26 traditional departments dissolved. 9 autonomous agent pools handle all execution work. Key human-only functions: legal accountability, fiduciary duty, relationship equity, regulatory standing.
