# Abzum Positioning

## Tagline

> **Abzum builds AI-native software 3-5× faster and 60-80% cheaper than traditional IT.**

## What We Sell

Two anchor products plus a tiered services catalogue:

### Products ([04-products/](../04-products/_index.md))

1. **[Abzum InterACT](../04-products/interact/_index.md)** — voice-activated, dynamically-rendered, multi-tenant CRM. Replaces traditional CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, Twenty) with an AI-native voice-first surface where the UI adapts to user intent in real-time. Multi-tenant with parent-child hierarchy supports MSPs / franchises / aggregators.

2. **[Abzum ReQuire](../04-products/require/_index.md)** — productized requirements elicitation. Conducts client discovery calls via voice, generates UI mockups live mid-conversation, produces structured requirements docs. Used internally for every project; sold to clients who want their own self-service discovery tool.

### Services ([03-services/](../03-services/_index.md))

17 v1 services across 7 categories: Build · Automation · Audit & Advisory · Creative & Content · Research & Knowledge · Managed/Ongoing · Future. Plus 13 catalogued v1.5/v2/v3+ services.

## Differentiation

- **Voice-first** — every client engagement starts with a voice discovery call (via Abzum ReQuire). Live mockups generated during the conversation. Structured requirements doc produced same-call.
- **AI-native** — 95% AI-agent organisation. 43 documented roles spanning exec / project-delivery / engineering / design / knowledge / platform / business-ops. Humans only for trust-critical sign-offs.
- **Hourly + per-project estimate pricing** — transparent, predictable, aligned with effort
- **Memory-stack proven** — Hindsight (91.4% LongMemEval) + LLM Wiki + ClickHouse + GitHub backup; institutional memory compounds over time
- **OpenCode-backed model portfolio** — BV $40/mo or BP $50/mo subscription stacks; optimised for cost/quality at every persona

## Target Market

- **Primary**: NZ + AU SMBs (5-50 staff) + startups
- **Secondary**: MSPs, franchises, aggregators (multi-tenant fit)
- **International**: SMB clients via Recall.ai voice infrastructure (90+ languages via Gemini 3.1 Flash Live)

## Competitive Position

| Competitor | What we beat them on |
|---|---|
| Traditional IT consultancies (Deloitte, IBM, Accenture) | 3-5× faster, 60-80% cheaper, voice-native intake |
| Boutique dev shops (local NZ/AU agencies) | AI-native (95% agent staffing), institutional memory, two-product moat |
| CRM SaaS (Salesforce, HubSpot, Twenty) | InterACT is voice-first + dynamic UI + flexible schema; their UIs are rigid |
| AI-tooling startups (cursor, devin, etc.) | We deliver finished outcomes, not just developer tools |

## Pricing Model (high-level)

**Hourly + per-project estimate** — see [`03-services/pricing.md`](../03-services/pricing.md). The legacy "≤$15K rule" is dropped (D21 2026-05-10). Some services range higher; CDO + Vijay sign off on engagements >$50K.

## Brand Voice

Sharp, efficient, resourceful. Felix Stanley (CAIO) is the public AI face for the company; sharp, professional, slightly playful. Vijay (CEO) is the founder voice — strategic, partnerships, vision.
