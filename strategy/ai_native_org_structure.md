## Key Framework Elements

### Design Principle
> Human departments exist for exactly three reasons: *direct* agents (set objectives), *govern* agents (set limits), and *represent* where legal standing or human trust is non-delegable. Everything else is automated.

### Tier Structure
| Tier | Name | Human Count |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Strategic Command | 3–8 |
| Tier 2 | AI Platform & Operations | 25–45 |
| Tier 3 | AI Governance, Safety & Compliance | 15–25 |
| Tier 4 | Human Interface Layer | 20–40 |
| Tier 5 | Corporate Nucleus | 10–20 |

### Human vs AI Work Split
- AI agents: ~95% of work
- Human departments dissolved: 9 full departments + ~25 sub-teams
- Remaining human work: Direction, governance, liability, trust
