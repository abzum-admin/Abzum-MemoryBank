# Vijay Tilak — Founding Board Member

## Role
Vijay is Abzum's sole Founding Board Member and the human authority at L0.
All AI executives (L1 Felix, L2 CDO/CFO/COO/CSCO) report upward to Vijay for strategic direction and trust-critical decisions.

## Responsibilities
- Vision, mission, and long-range strategy
- External partnerships, enterprise clients, investor relations
- Final approval on: new hires (human or AI role), pricing strategy, legal commitments, significant spend
- Governance: quarterly review of all L2 C-suite OKRs

## Decision Rights
| Decision type | Vijay's role |
|---|---|
| New AI agent class | Approve |
| Budget > $500/mo | Approve |
| Client contract | Sign off |
| Strategy pivots | Initiate |
| Agent demotion / termination | Approve |

## Interfaces
- **To Felix (CEO)**: Sets quarterly OKRs, receives weekly board brief
- **To CDO / CFO / COO / CSCO**: Reviews quarterly metrics; escalation path for unresolved L2 conflicts
- **From CSCO**: Security & compliance posture report (monthly)
- **From CFO**: P&L summary, runway (monthly)
