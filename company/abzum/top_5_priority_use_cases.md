## Narrative
### Structure
Five priority use cases ranked by urgency, market gap, and AI suitability. UC-01 is the anchor product.

### Dependencies
Market gap between spreadsheets and enterprise CRMs drives UC-01. Data silos (5-15 tools) drive UC-16.

### Highlights
UC-01 Custom CRM is the first product. Automation and integration are fast wins. Chatbot (UC-20) enables lower-cost entry demonstrating AI-first differentiation.

### Rules
Rule 1: Use cases must be deliverable at -15K price point
Rule 2: 2-4 week delivery window
Rule 3: Must be testable/rule-based for automation use cases
